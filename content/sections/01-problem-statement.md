# 1. Problem statement

At EPAM, we see strong customer demand for GenAI solutions in general and Talk-to-your-Data applications in particular. However, when teams need to move from POC to MVP, they often face similar challenges – especially when the goal is to demonstrate business value and reliability.

Typical challenges include (but are not limited to):

- **Accuracy and reliability:** Business users may expect deterministic, consistently correct answers, while GenAI apps cannot reliably answer arbitrary, open-ended questions without the right context and constraints.
- **Limited delivery experience:** Teams new to GenAI may not fully understand what it takes to build a production-ready solution (beyond a demo-quality POC).
- **Vague business goals and success metrics:** Projects often lack clear targets for metrics such as accuracy, usability and ROI – making stakeholder alignment difficult.
- **Performance and cost:** Latency and usage-based costs can become blockers, especially as solutions scale and adopt multi-step or multi-agent patterns.
