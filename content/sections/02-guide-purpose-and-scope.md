This guide is a practical, high-level quick start for EPAM project teams delivering GenAI solutions (especially Talk-to-your-Data applications) when key team members may lack hands-on experience moving from POC to MVP. It outlines technology-agnostic principles focused on the business and organizational practices that drive adoption, reliability, and measurable value (e.g., expectation management, context engineering ownership, evaluation, data product readiness, ROI framing, and incremental delivery).

**Out of scope:**

- Detailed technical design and implementation guidance (architecture patterns, code-level frameworks, integration details).
- GenAI MLOps / operationalization practices (e.g., monitoring, governance tooling, CI/CD for prompts/models/agents), which are addressed in a separate document.
